Description

Restaurant Tycoon is a 3D restaurant simulation game where players manage and grow their own restaurant. In the game, players can organize the restaurant space, serve customers, earn money, and improve the business through upgrades. The main goal is to create an enjoyable restaurant experience while balancing customer needs, restaurant layout, and business growth. Players will interact with the environment, complete tasks, and make decisions that help the restaurant become more successful over time. The project focuses on simulation gameplay, simple management mechanics, and a visually interactive 3D space. Restaurant Tycoon is designed to be easy to understand while still giving players a sense of progress, creativity, and responsibility as they build their restaurant from the ground up.
